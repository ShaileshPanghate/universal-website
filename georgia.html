Why Choose MBBS in Georgia?

🏛️ European Standard Education

Georgian medical universities follow European curriculum standards with modern teaching methodologies, advanced simulation labs, and emphasis on practical skills development.

💰 Affordable Tuition Fees

Compared to Western countries, Georgia offers quality medical education at just $4,000-$7,000 per year with low living costs ($200-$300/month).

🌍 Globally Recognized Degrees

Medical degrees from Georgia are recognized by WHO, NMC, ECFMG, FAIMER, and medical councils worldwide, enabling graduates to practice internationally.

🏥 Early Clinical Exposure

Students gain hands-on experience from 2nd/3rd year in university-affiliated hospitals with diverse patient cases and modern medical equipment.

🛂 Easy Admission Process

No entrance exams required (only 50% in PCB for most universities). Direct admission with minimal documentation and high visa approval rate.

🏔️ Safe & Beautiful Country

Georgia offers a safe European environment with stunning landscapes, rich culture, and welcoming people. English is widely spoken in university areas.
