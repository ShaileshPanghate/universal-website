Frequently Asked Questions

Is MBBS from China valid in my home country?

Yes, Chinese medical degrees are recognized by WHO and most medical councils worldwide including MCI (India), PMDC (Pakistan), GMC (UK), etc. Graduates need to pass the respective licensing examination (like FMGE, USMLE, PLAB) to practice in their home country.

What is the duration of MBBS in China?

The MBBS program in China is 6 years duration, including 1 year of internship. Some universities offer 5+1 year programs where the internship can be done in your home country.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, the Chinese government and universities offer various scholarships like CSC Scholarship, Provincial Scholarships, and University Scholarships covering 20-100% of tuition fees based on academic merit.

How is the accommodation for international students?

Universities provide comfortable hostel accommodation with options for single or shared rooms. Facilities typically include WiFi, laundry, common kitchen, study rooms, and 24/7 security.

What is the medium of instruction?

All MBBS programs for international students are taught entirely in English. Chinese language classes are mandatory in first 2 years to help with hospital interactions and daily life.
