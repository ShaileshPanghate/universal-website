Country-Specific Visa Requirements

Financial Requirements

  • Bank statement showing ₹5-8 lakhs balance
  • Sponsorship letter if applicable
  • University fee payment receipt

Medical Requirements

  • HIV test report (not older than 3 months)
  • Chest X-ray report
  • COVID vaccination certificate

Processing Time

  • Normal processing: 15-20 working days
  • Express processing: 7-10 working days (extra fee)
  • Interview: Usually not required

Financial Requirements

  • Bank statement showing ₹6-10 lakhs balance
  • Sponsorship affidavit
  • Tuition fee payment proof

Medical Requirements

  • Physical Examination Record (Form JW202)
  • Blood test reports
  • ECG report

Processing Time

  • Normal processing: 4-6 weeks
  • Interview: Occasionally required
  • Visa type: X1/X2 Student Visa

Visa Document Checklist

Complete list of documents we help you prepare for student visa

  • Original passport (minimum 18 months validity)
  • 2 photocopies of passport first & last page
  • Birth certificate (translated if not in English)
  • 8 passport-size photographs (35x45mm, white background)
  • 10th & 12th mark sheets and certificates (attested)
  • NEET scorecard (for Indian students)
  • University admission letter (original)
  • Invitation letter from university (for some countries)
  • Bank statement (last 6 months, minimum balance varies)
  • Sponsorship letter (if applicable)
  • Income tax returns (parents/guardian, last 3 years)
  • Fixed deposit certificates (if showing as financial proof)
  • HIV test report (from approved labs)
  • Chest X-ray report (for some countries)
  • COVID vaccination certificate
  • General medical fitness certificate
