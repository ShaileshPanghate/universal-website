Why Study MBBS in Philippines?

The Philippines is one of the top destinations for Indian and international students seeking a high-quality, affordable, and globally recognized MBBS degree. With English as the medium of instruction, world-class universities, and a curriculum similar to the US medical education system, the Philippines provides an excellent pathway to becoming a successful doctor.

Affordable Fees

No donation or capitation fees with transparent fee structure that's much lower than Indian private colleges.

Global Recognition

Degrees approved by WHO, NMC (MCI), ECFMG, FAIMER, making you eligible to practice worldwide.

US-Based Curriculum

Prepares students for USMLE, PLAB, and NEXT exams with teaching methods matching international standards.

English Medium

No language barrier as English is the primary medium of instruction and communication.

High Success Rate

Excellent FMGE/NEXT passing rates compared to many other foreign medical universities.

Indian Community

Large Indian student population making adaptation easier with Indian food options available.

Eligibility Criteria

Requirements for MBBS in Philippines:

  • Academic Qualification: 10+2 with PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and at least 50% marks
  • NEET Qualification: Mandatory for Indian students (since 2018)
  • English Proficiency: No IELTS/TOEFL required (Medium of instruction is English)
  • Age Limit: Minimum 17 years at the time of admission
  • Documents Required: Passport, Academic Certificates, NEET Scorecard, Birth Certificate, Medical Certificate

Admission Process

Step 1: Application Submission

Submit scanned copies of your academic documents, passport, and NEET scorecard to the university or through our portal.

Step 2: Admission Letter

Receive offer letter from the university within 2-4 weeks after document verification.

Step 3: Visa Processing

Apply for student visa (9F Visa) with our guidance - takes approximately 4-6 weeks.

Step 4: Travel & Enrollment

Fly to Philippines and complete university enrollment formalities to start your MBBS journey!
