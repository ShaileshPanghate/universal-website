Simple Admission Process

1

Check Eligibility

Minimum 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in 12th grade. NEET qualification required for Indian students (for MCI registration).

2

Submit Documents

10th & 12th mark sheets, passport copy, birth certificate, medical certificate, and application form with 8 passport photos.

3

Receive Admission Letter

Get official admission confirmation within 2-3 weeks after document verification by the university.

4

Apply for Student Visa

University provides visa support documents. Submit at Kyrgyz embassy with passport and required fees.

5

Travel & Enrollment

Fly to Kyrgyzstan, complete university registration, medical check-up, and residence permit formalities.

Life as a Medical Student in Kyrgyzstan

Kyrgyzstan offers a safe, welcoming environment for international students with affordable living costs and beautiful natural surroundings. The country has four distinct seasons with spectacular mountain views.

Universities provide comfortable hostel accommodation with Indian food options available. The local people are friendly and English is widely understood in university areas.

🏠

Accommodation

Hostel fees: $800-1200/year with options for shared or private rooms

🍛

Food

Monthly food expenses: $150-200 with Indian restaurants near campuses

🚍

Transportation

Efficient public transport with student discounts, monthly pass: $10-15
