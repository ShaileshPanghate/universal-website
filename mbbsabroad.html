Eligibility Criteria

Requirements to pursue MBBS abroad through Universal Education Consultancy

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum 50% marks in 10+2 (PCB) for general category
  • 40% marks for reserved categories (varies by country)
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology as main subjects
  • English as a compulsory subject
  • NEET qualification (for Indian students)
  • Minimum 17 years of age

Document Requirements

  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
  • NEET scorecard (for Indian students)
  • Passport with minimum 18 months validity
  • Birth certificate
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Passport size photographs
  • Statement of purpose
  • Letters of recommendation (if required)

Other Requirements

  • English proficiency test (IELTS/TOEFL for some countries)
  • Financial proof for tuition and living expenses
  • Police clearance certificate
  • HIV test reports (for some countries)
  • COVID vaccination certificate (if required)
