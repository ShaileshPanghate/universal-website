Our Post-Arrival Services

We provide end-to-end support to help you settle in and focus on your medical studies.

Airport Reception

Warm welcome at the airport with our representatives who will escort you to your accommodation and help with initial settlement.

Accommodation Assistance

Help with finding suitable housing near your university, whether dormitories, shared apartments, or homestays.

University Orientation

Comprehensive campus tour and introduction to university facilities, departments, and important contacts.

Bank Account Setup

Assistance with opening a local bank account and understanding international money transfer processes.

Mobile & Internet

Help with getting local SIM cards, mobile plans, and setting up internet services in your accommodation.

Health Insurance

Guidance on health insurance requirements and assistance with registration with local healthcare providers.

Ongoing Student Support

Our support continues throughout your medical education journey with these essential services:

  • Academic Support

    Access to senior student mentors, study materials, and guidance on academic requirements.

  • Cultural Adaptation

    Regular cultural events and guidance to help you adjust to your new environment.

  • Visa Renewal Assistance

    Help with visa extensions and documentation for continuous legal stay.

  • Travel Assistance

    Support with holiday travel planning and airport transfers during breaks.
