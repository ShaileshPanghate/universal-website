About Universal Education Consultants

Pioneers in Overseas Medical Admissions

For over two decades, Universal Education Consultants (UEC) has been the premier choice for students seeking quality medical education abroad. We specialize in guiding students to premier universities in Russia, Romania, Kyrgyzstan, Philippines, Kazakhstan, Bangladesh, Nepal, Uzbekistan, and Ukraine.

As officially authorized representatives of leading medical universities, we provide end-to-end admission support for MBBS, BDS, and MD programs recognized by NMC/MCI, WHO, UNESCO, and the Dental Council of India.

Our transparent process, with no hidden donations or capitation fees, has helped thousands of students achieve their dream of becoming doctors. We handle everything from university selection to visa processing and post-arrival support.
